My top 3 movies of all time

KGF

*KGF* (Kolar Gold Fields) is an action-packed film about Rocky, who rises from poverty to control a powerful gold mine. Directed by Prashanth Neel, the movie is known for its intense action scenes and strong performances, especially by Yash. It became a huge hit across India, praised for its gripping story and raw energy.

BAAHUBALI

*Baahubali*, directed by S.S. Rajamouli, is a grand epic that tells the story of two brothers fighting for the throne of an ancient kingdom. The movie is famous for its breathtaking visuals and powerful characters, especially Prabhas as the hero. *Baahubali* became a cultural phenomenon, setting new standards for Indian cinema.

RRR

*RRR*, also directed by S.S. Rajamouli, is a fictional tale of two real-life freedom fighters, Alluri Sitarama Raju and Komaram Bheem, set during British rule. The film is known for its spectacular action scenes, powerful performances by Ram Charan and Jr. NTR, and its portrayal of friendship and patriotism. *RRR* received global acclaim for its entertainment value and stunning visuals.
